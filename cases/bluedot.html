Bluedot Redesign / Role. UIUX Designer

Project Background

The Bluedot Group comes from a strong lineage of emergency medical care. Our founders have spent decades fine-tuning medical transfer logistics by land and air. They have also been at the forefront of emergency medical care, both as physicians and as managers of multilingual, global teams.

Empathize

Why it needs a redesign?

After a careful analysis of the Bluedot website, I drew the following conclusion of why it needed a redesign:

1) The website looks outdated.

2) The CTA is not visible as it is wrapped by destructive elements.

3) There was no trust building up when the user sees it for the first time.

4)The brand values of the company were not clearly visible on the website.

5)Finds it hard to navigate through diffrent services.

Design architecture

I decided to design individual landing pages within the single domain based on user requirements and the corresponding business offerings. The home page primarily serves as informative section which promotes the services provided by the bluedot group and it is the major touch point for the web traffic. and also served as the trigger for other landing pages as well. So that there is something for all targeted user groups to get hooked on to and the system guides them to their corresponding landing page for a custom tailored experience.

Since bluedot serves to high end customers so the expectations and requirements will be pretty professional. So each of the services was broken into multiple landing pages to showcase the business offerings in depth so the customer will not feel lost,

Visual design

This was an area where i wanted to revamp the most. Initially, Multiple graphic styles were tried to find the right combination of graphics and content. Finally went with the clean minimalistic design pattern with a lot of white space in combination with a few bright colors to represente the brand identity of bluedot. . The hero images for each landing pages looked lively and was perfectly communicating the services bluedot group offers to the respective user group.
