Boomify / (Lollypop studio design Challange)

Project Background

Boomify is an app that encourages and educates people to follow a plastic free lifestyle.

problem

The world is facing a plastic crisis. Plastic pollution has become a global concern, as our planet is drowning in plastic litter and microplastics. While plastic has many valuable uses, societies have become highly dependent on single-use or disposable plastic — with severe environmental consequences.

Opportunity

Why don't we create a digital solution where we can encourage people to reduce their dependency on single-use or disposable plastic usage.

Boomify aims to educate people on the importance and impact of reducing plastic and rewarding them for cutting back on their plastic usage. Users will be able to gain points as a reward by participating in quizzes, reading articles, and buying environment-friendly products.

User research

Due to time constraints, I started my research by going through the analogous apps which offer similar services to see how they do in the market and the features they offer. and conducted online research to see how we can leverage technology to reduce plastic pollution and how recycling helps.

“Only 9% of all plastic produced by humanity has been recycled and only 14% of plastic waste is currently being collected for recycling, and it is increasingly seen as having huge potential to tackle the plastic crisis. However, plastic recycling presents huge challenges due to the nature of the material itself.”

The focus of my app was to educate people about the environmental consequences caused by the plastic pollution and create opportunities to be part of sustainable life and contribute to the climate change problem by promoting environment-friendly products.

As part of defining my target audience, I went through a couple of surveys that indicate the age group of people who consumes a large number of single-use or disposable plastic materials.

after analyzing the survey insight I have decided to go with a visualized dashboard or similar patterns where we show the amount of impact that a person’s reduction of plastic is making on our environment. for encouraging them to do more I introduced a reward system where their actions within the app such as participating on quizzes, removing plastic material from their day to day life environment and buying environment-friendly products help earn points that add up to an overall reward.
