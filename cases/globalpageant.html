Global Pageant / Role. Product Designer (UX/UI)

Project Background

Global pageant is a professional third-party inspection and testing company headquartered in Dubai, UAE. With a unique global presence and solid industry expertise, Global pageant stretches support for projects in more than 45 countries.

Challange

Global pageant wanted to build a marketplace application for inspectors who are looking for a freelance or full-time jobs and for their customers who require inspection for their company the challenge of this project was to solve the problems of job seeking inspection professionals by eliminating the the tedious process of finding the genuine clients, and for the customers they can save time and the frustrating process of going through several ordinary job portals to find the right inspectors, as the inspection professionals are prescreened by an experienced global pageant team and it is guaranteed that each inspector profile contains relevant and verified pieces of information.

Project Goals

The primary goal of this project is to create a marketplace app that helps inspection professionals find freelance or full-time jobs. and for the customers to find the inspection services.

My Role

As the sole designer for this project, I was responsible for the entire process, that is, the research, user flow, wireframing, designing, and prototyping.

Design Process

Research Approach

To better understand the challenges that inspection professionals face, I decided to begin my research process by reaching out to few inspectors and engineer graduates. By asking for their feed back via short questionnnaire. Here are some of the question they were asked:

1. Do you have any working experience in the inspection industry?
2. What resources would you use to look for a job?
3. What details do you prefer to see before applying for a job post?
4. Are you comfortable giving interviews online?
5. How reliable is an online platform to get a job?
6. Where do you look for a job often?
7. What platform do you think is the most reliable to find inspection jobs?

In analyzing their responses, I realized they shared one major pain point and that is, that there is no dedicated job portal or marketplace app for inspection jobs. I also got a few comments on how grateful it would be if there is a platform where they get training as part of the hiring processes before they land on the job. Next, I searched to find out if there were any platforms in existence that are meant to solve the same problem, however, I could not find a single app for the same but there were very few websites which offer contract and full-time inspection jobs that came with so many limitations, I then narrowed them to 2 platforms I felt came the close to being competitors even though they don't have a mobile app yet.
