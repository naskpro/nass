Project Background

Global pageant is a professional third-party inspection and testing company headquartered in Dubai, UAE. With a unique global presence and solid industry expertise, Global pageant stretches support for projects in more than 45 countries.

Challange

Global pageant wanted to build a marketplace application for inspectors who are looking for a freelance or full-time jobs and for their customers who require inspection for their company the challenge of this project was to solve the problems of job seeking inspection professionals by eliminating the the tedious process of finding the genuine clients, and for the customers they can save time and the frustrating process of going through several ordinary job portals to find the right inspectors, as the inspection professionals are prescreened by an experienced global pageant team and it is guaranteed that each inspector profile contains relevant and verified pieces of information.

Project Goals

The primary goal of this project is to create a marketplace app that helps inspection professionals find freelance or full-time jobs.

Design Process

Research Approach

To better understand the challenges that inspection professionals face, I decided to first dive into customer interviews. By asking open-ended questions, I explored different job concerns they have, what goals they strive for, and what other platforms they use to find jobs/clients.

The goals for my research process were the following:

Understand the market of platforms related to books

Explore and identify the job process of inspection professionals and how they
search for jobs.

Identify pain points of finding inspection jobs from the regular job portals.

Understand the market of platforms related to jobs.

Customer Interviews Findings

“Three out of four participants think there is no dedicated
marketplace for inspection professionals to find
genuine clients.”

“Two out of four participants said job descriptions on the
regular job portals are either complex or irrelevant.”

User Persona

After getting insights from research, I created personas that represented our potential target users and captured their needs and frustrations.

User Flow

I kept the user flow very minimal so that the product will be ease of use and have better accessibility.
